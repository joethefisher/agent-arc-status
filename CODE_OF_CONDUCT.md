# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be direct, be respectful, and attack ideas rather than people. We want a high signal-to-noise ratio on every thread.

In practice:

- Critique designs and claims as sharply as you like — keep it about the work.
- Assume good faith. Ask before concluding someone is wrong or acting in bad faith.
- No harassment, personal attacks, demeaning language, or discrimination.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel that represents the project — and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the private contact, once configured). The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this standard, and may block repeat offenders.
